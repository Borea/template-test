What are Medford knives?

Medford Knives

Medford knives are high-quality, American-made knives that are built to last. Each knife is handmade in Medford, Oregon, and is designed for both everyday use and hard-use applications. Medford knives are made from a variety of materials including stainless steel, tool steel, and Damascus steel. Whether you’re looking for a new EDC (everyday carry) knife or a tough tactical knife, Medford has a blade that will fit your needs.

The History of Medford knives

Medford knives was founded in 1997 by knife maker Greg Medford. Medford’s goal was to create a line of knives that combined the best features of American and European knives. Over the years, Medford has continued to innovate and now offers a wide range of knives for both everyday use and hard-use applications. Whether you’re looking for a sleek folder or a rugged fixed blade, Medford has a knife that will fit your needs.

If you’re looking for a high-quality, American-made knife, then Medford is the brand for you. With a focus on attention to detail and innovative design, Medford knives are built to last.

The different types of Medford knives

Medford offers a wide range of knives, from sleek folders to tough fixed blades. Here’s a quick rundown of the different types of Medford knives:

Folders: Medford folders are designed for everyday carry (EDC). They’re usually smaller in size and have a variety of blade shapes and handle materials.

Fixed blades: Medford fixed blades are designed for hard-use applications. They’re usually larger in size and have a full tang construction for extra strength and durability.

Tactical knives: Medford tactical knives are designed for self-defense and law enforcement applications. They often have features like serrated edges and glass breaker tips.

Custom knives: Medford also offers a wide range of custom knives. You can choose from a variety of blade shapes, handle materials, and finishes to create a truly unique knife.

How to care for your Medford knife

To keep your Medford knife in good condition, it’s important to clean and oil it regularly. Medford recommends cleaning your knife with mild soap and warm water. You can then dry it with a soft cloth or air dry it. Once it’s dry, you can apply a light coat of oil to the blade and handle. Medford also recommends that you store your knife in a cool, dry place.

Medford knives are built to last, but it’s still important to take care of them. With regular cleaning and oiling, your Medford knife will continue to perform at its best.

Medford Knives Sharpening Tips

As you use your Medford knife, the blade will gradually become dull. When this happens, it’s important to sharpen your knife to keep it performing at its best. Medford recommends using a sharpening stone to sharpen your knife. First, choose a stone that’s appropriate for the type of steel in your knife. Medford knives are made from a variety of steels, so you’ll need to use a different stone for each type of steel. Second, choose a grit that’s appropriate for the level of sharpness you want to achieve. Medford recommends starting with coarse grit and then progressing to finer grit. Finally, follow the instructions that come with your sharpening stone.

Sharpening your knife regularly will keep it performing at its best. With the right sharpening stone, it’s easy to keep your Medford knife razor-sharp.
